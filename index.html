About Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was a prominent leader of the Indian independence movement. He is known for his philosophy of nonviolent resistance, which he called "Satyagraha". Gandhi's actions and teachings played a significant role in India's struggle for freedom from British rule.

Famous Quotes

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others." - Mahatma Gandhi

"You must be the change you want to see in the world." - Mahatma Gandhi
